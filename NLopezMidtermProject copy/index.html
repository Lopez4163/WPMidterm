Chef Persy

Peruvian Chef

Chef Jose Persy, hailing from Jaén, Peru, brings a deep-rooted passion for northern Peruvian cuisine, with a culinary style shaped by the Amazon’s flavors and traditions. Known for his mastery of regional dishes like juane, a savory blend of rice and chicken wrapped in jungle leaves, and tacacho con cuyi, a unique dish combining mashed plantains with roasted guinea pig, Chef Persy has dedicated his career to celebrating the authentic tastes of his homeland. His culinary excellence has earned him accolades throughout Peru, where he’s recognized for both preserving and elevating traditional flavors, making him a celebrated ambassador of Amazonian and highland Peruvian gastronomy.

Chef Camilo

Colombian Chef

Chef Juan Camilo, a native of Cali, Colombia, infuses his cooking with the vibrant flavors and cultural essence of his hometown. Renowned for his expertise in preparing traditional dishes like sancocho de gallina, a hearty chicken stew rich with local ingredients like plantains, yuca, and corn, Chef Juan also brings to life Cali’s iconic pandebono, a warm cheese bread beloved across Colombia. With a deep appreciation for the Afro-Colombian and indigenous influences that define Cali's cuisine, Chef Juan Camilo has become a culinary force, recognized for his dedication to preserving and sharing Colombia’s rich food heritage, with a special focus on the rhythms and flavors of his birthplace.
