Chef Persy

Peruvian Chef

Chef Jose Persy, from Jaén, Peru, has a deep passion for northern Peruvian cuisine, influenced by the Amazon’s rich flavors and traditions. He specializes in regional dishes like juane, a savory rice and chicken blend wrapped in jungle leaves, and tacacho con cuyi, mashed plantains served with roasted guinea pig. Celebrated for his culinary excellence, Chef Persy is recognized as an ambassador of Amazonian and highland Peruvian gastronomy, dedicated to preserving and elevating traditional flavors.

Chef Camilo

Colombian Chef

Chef Juan Camilo, from Cali, Colombia, infuses his cooking with the vibrant flavors of his hometown. He is renowned for traditional dishes like sancocho de gallina, a hearty chicken stew with local ingredients, and pandebono, a beloved cheese bread. With a deep appreciation for the Afro-Colombian and indigenous influences that shape Cali's cuisine, Chef Juan is recognized for his dedication to preserving and sharing Colombia’s rich food heritage.

Where To Find Us?

Located in the heart of where Colombia meets Peru, the cultural neighborhood of Jackson Heights. Get off the 7 train at 82nd Street, and you are only a 5-minute walk from our establishment. We chose to start our legacy here to fuse Colombian and Peruvian cuisine in the heart of Colombian and Peruvian culture.
